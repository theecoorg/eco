Harnessing Creativity for Climate Action

We believe art and technology has the power to change the world

The Eco Organization is an initiative that aims to find ways to solve the climate crisis through the use of art and innovation. Through collaborative partnerships, we seek to bring together like-minded artists and innovators around the world to become a driving force that can make an impact on the future of our planet.

We will be leveraging our network to explore and support innovative projects focused on protecting and preserving the environment. Our activities will be centered around the Sustainable Development Goals set by the United Nations. Our founding team is made up of teams and institutes from the Maldives, UK, Switzerland, Netherlands, Germany, and the US.

Our Initiatives

1. ECTOPIA

A blueprint for the future of civilization

Ectopia is a blueprint of a self-sustainable state administered through the use of advanced technology. Ectopia’s aim is to build new habitats for environmentally challenged communities and achieve the Sustainable Development Goals set by the United Nations.

The project is based on the open-source Ectopia framework by Hilal Agil. We expect contributions from the international community to help advance the project and bring the concept to reality.
