Harnessing Creativity for Climate Action

We believe art and technology has the power to change the world

The impacts of climate change are already being felt

The Eco Organization is an initiative that aims to find ways to solve the climate crisis through the use of art and innovation. Through collaborative partnerships, we seek to bring together like-minded artists and innovators around the world to become a driving force that can make an impact on the future of our planet.

We will be leveraging our network to explore and support innovative projects focused on protecting and preserving the environment. Our activities will be centered around the Sustainable Development Goals set by the United Nations. Multiple individuals, teams and institutes from the Maldives, UK, Switzerland, Netherlands, Belgium, Spain, Germany, the US, are currently contributing to our initiatives.

Ectopia

A blueprint for habitats of the future.

Ectopia is a blueprint of a self-sustainable, location independent state administered through advanced technology. Ectopia’s aim is to build new sustainable habitats and achieve the Sustainable Development Goals set by the United Nations.
